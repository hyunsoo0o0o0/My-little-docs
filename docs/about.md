# About SSTDV-Project

SSTDV-Project (Secure and Sustainable Technology Development Project) is a research initiative focused on developing open-source software tools for scientific computing, machine learning applications, and data analysis across various domains including medical imaging, environmental science, and computational physics.

## Research Areas

### Medical Imaging & Computational Anatomy

Development of tools for analyzing medical imaging data, including brain structure analysis, appearance modeling, and synthetic data generation for training robust machine learning models.

**Related Projects**: BrainShapeToolKit, DiffAM, DiffuAug, VCM

### Environmental Science & Oceanography

Modeling and analysis of environmental data including sea surface temperature, extreme value analysis for climate research, and fluid dynamics simulation.

**Related Projects**: KIOST-SST-EVL, python-fluid-simulation

### Generative Models & Synthetic Data

Research into generative models for creating high-quality synthetic data, with emphasis on coverage control, distribution matching, and applications in data-scarce domains.

**Related Projects**: HF-GAN, DiffuAug, VCM, EvalDistSim
